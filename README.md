Reserved is an Online Room Reservation Application suitable for Multi Location & Multi Property Hotel Chains, Big & Small Single Property Hotels & Resorts, Tourist Lodges, Guest Houses, Home Stays and Hostels. The tech stack includes:
  <li>PHP 7.4 and above</li>
  <li>MySQL</li>
  <li>Javascript & JQuery 3.6</li>
  <li>Bootstrap 4 & 5</li>
  <li>Gulp & Saas</li>
